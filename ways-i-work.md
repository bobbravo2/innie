# Ways That I Work

I wrote this document to help my team get to know me better.

## Operating System

I use macOS as my daily driver for development.

## Calendar

My work calendar is public to all employees. You can see the details of the
events that I attend. You are welcome to make the same change to your calendar.
I find this spreads information async around the team.

## Slack

I set aside one focus day each week where I sign out of Slack. I use this time
to concentrate on sprint goals and planned work. I will still check email
periodically.

If something is urgent and important, my manager can reach me.

I do not have Slack or work email installed on my phone.
